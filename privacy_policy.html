Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HabitPaper ("we", "our", or "app") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, display name, and avatar URL from Google. This is used to create and manage your account.
  • Profile Data. Your user ID and premium subscription status are stored on our backend (Supabase) to manage feature access.

1.2 Locally Stored Information

The following data is stored only on your device using local storage (Hive) and is never sent to our servers:

  • Habits, goals, and daily completion records
  • Water intake tracking data
  • Wallpaper preferences and overlay widget settings
  • App theme selection
  • Haptic feedback preference

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Advertising ID. If ad personalization is enabled on your device, Google Mobile Ads may collect your advertising ID to serve personalized ads. You can reset or opt out of this at any time in your device settings.
  • Usage Data. We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics. No third-party analytics SDKs are used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you via Google Sign-In
  • To manage your premium subscription status
  • To display non-personalized or personalized advertisements
  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent — when you agree to our privacy policy and terms of service
  • Contractual necessity — to provide the services you request (e.g., managing your account and subscription)
  • Legitimate interests — to operate and secure the app

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — for user profile and premium status storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — for processing in-app purchases and managing subscription status. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — for serving advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — for processing subscription payments. Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account profile data (user ID, premium status) is stored securely on Supabase servers. All other data (habits, water intake, preferences) is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen. Upon deletion, your profile data will be removed from our backend. Locally stored data can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings. Subscription and purchase records are retained by RevenueCat and Google Play as required for billing and tax purposes. If you want to delete your account or request that your collected data be removed, you can also send an email directly to us at surajsays@proton.me . We will process your deletion request within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

7.1 GDPR (European Economic Area)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
